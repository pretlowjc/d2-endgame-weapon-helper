DPS of Primary Weapon Types

What is this?

This chart is to show a comparison of each primary weapon type and compare their dps numbers against each other. This will provide us with legimate numbers and proof of what weapons are better, viable, and others to simply not touch unless other circumstances arise.

True DPS: This number is the amount of damage in one single magazine. Calculating this is rather simple except when it comes to certain weapons such as shotguns which are not shown.

Sustained DPS: This number is given taking reloading into consideration. This number is a bit more useful in my opinion. During a DPS phase or while damaging enemies that can take more damage you will want a weapon that has a higher number for sustained dps.
